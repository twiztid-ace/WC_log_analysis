Per-boss summary

HPS and overheal are real, computed from Crowns's own fight logs. Percentile is the real WCL ranking for each exact pull — click a boss name for the full audit.

!
Flash of Light is the primary heal on 7 of 10 bosses, where Top 10 parses on the same encounters use Holy Light as primary on 8 of 10. The gap is most extreme on Hydross the Unstable (89.9% Flash of Light for Crowns vs 96.4% Holy Light for the Top 10 field — essentially inverted) and High Astromancer Solarian (98.2% vs 94.7%, in the other direction). The two fights where this doesn't apply — Lady Vashj and Kael'thas Sunstrider — are exactly the two fights where the Top 10 field itself shifts toward Flash of Light for movement reasons, so Crowns's choice tracks the benchmark there. This pattern shows up on every boss page below and is the single most consistent, most explanatory finding of the raid night — it lines up closely with the lowest-percentile fights (Morogrim, Hydross).
