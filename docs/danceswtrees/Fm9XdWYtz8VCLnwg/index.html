Four real patterns hold up across the 9 real kills: HPS trails the Top 100 average on every single kill, no exceptions. Overheal exceeds the Top 100 sample's single worst parse on 5 of 9 kills (Lurker, Leotheras, Karathress, Void Reaver, Solarian) — a real, recurring gap on more than half the night, not one-off bad luck. Lifebloom ran hotter than its own Top 100 benchmark share on all 9 kills, but the size of that gap varies enormously by boss — from a modest +7.5 points on Solarian and +8.2 on Vashj up to +65.8 on Void Reaver, +47.3 on Leotheras, and +46.1 on Karathress (three of the last four kills of the night). Void Reaver stands out specifically because the sample's own approach there is Regrowth-dominant (75.6%), not Lifebloom-led like every other boss — Danceswtrees's real cast mix doesn't adapt to that. Solarian is the one kill where Danceswtrees's own dominant spell (Regrowth, 65.7%) actually matches the sample's own dominant spell there (59.6%), even though Lifebloom is still individually overweighted. Tree of Life saw real, heavy use on only 2 of 9 kills (Vashj 93.7%, Solarian 100%) and read a flat 0% on the other 7 — a consistent, worth-investigating pattern rather than fight-by-fight noise.
