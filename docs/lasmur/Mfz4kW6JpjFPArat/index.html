Two consistent, raid-wide patterns stand out. First, Blessing of Life - a self-cast the Top 100 sample uses on only 3-8% of parses per boss - was cast on 8 of 10 kills, the most consistent cooldown deviation of the raid. Second, Circle of Healing undershot the Top 100 sample's share on 8 of 10 kills, with Renew, Prayer of Mending, and Power Word: Shield making up the difference; Power Word: Shield's own near-0% Top 100 usage rate on most bosses is a known benchmark bias, not a real norm, so its low usage isn't read as underuse anywhere in this report. Leotheras the Blind was by far the deadliest kill of the raid (22 deaths, more than double the next-highest kill), and Lasmur died personally 3 times during it, plus 2 more times on Morogrim Tidewalker - the only two kills flagged for self-deaths this raid. Solarian's 9 deaths landed in a tight ~12-second burst rather than spread across the pull. The three weakest kills by percentile were Lurker (12th), Morogrim (21st), and Solarian (22nd); Kael'thas Sunstrider was the strongest (71st).
