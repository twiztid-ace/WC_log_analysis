All 12 real bosses attempted were killed this raid night, including the Gruul's Lair pair (High King Maulgar, Gruul the Dragonkiller). This was a genuinely mixed raid night: Vajomee ranked 1st of the tracked healers present by item-level rank on only 2 of 12 kills (46% average rank percentile) and 1st by raw healing on just 1 of 12 - a notably weaker showing than Vajomee's other recent reports. Percentiles ranged widely, from a very low 9th (Al'ar, the lowest of any kill in any Vajomee report this session) to 81st (Gruul the Dragonkiller) - Al'ar, Kael'thas (18th), and Lady Vashj (30th) were the raid's three lowest kills. The single most consistent real pattern across all 12 kills: the Top 100 benchmark favors Chain Heal's highest real rank (guid 25423, 540 mana) on every boss (55-87% of parses, depending on the fight), while Vajomee cast meaningfully more of the cheaper lower rank (guid 10623, 405 mana) on every single kill - from a near-match on Gruul (76.1% high-rank vs. the benchmark's 77.6%) to an almost complete reversal on Lady Vashj (7.4% high-rank vs. the benchmark's 48.4%) and Void Reaver (15.9% vs. 83.9%). Two real self-deaths occurred this raid night (Lady Vashj, Kael'thas), and deaths were otherwise heaviest on Leotheras (9, the most of the raid night). The Lurker Below and Al'ar were the only two clean kills. Leotheras was the one kill with no Battle or Guardian Elixir active at all; Hydross and Morogrim were separately missing the Well Fed food buff despite having both elixirs up.
