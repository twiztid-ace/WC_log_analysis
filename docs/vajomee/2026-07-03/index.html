Per-boss summary

HPS and overheal are real, computed from Vajomee's own events-based fight logs. Percentile is the exact report/fight rank pulled from WCL's v2 API — click a boss name for the full audit.

!
Overheal exceeds the Top 100 sample's single worst real parse on 7 of 10 kills (Hydross, The Lurker Below, Morogrim Tidewalker, Lady Vashj, Al'ar, Void Reaver, High Astromancer Solarian) — the most consistent, raid-wide finding of the night, and consistent with the same pattern already documented on Vajomee's other raid night (2026-07-10, 6 of 9 kills there). Ancestral Swiftness went completely uncast on 5 of 10 kills (Hydross, Leotheras, Karathress, Lady Vashj, Void Reaver), each a boss where 80-96% of the Top 100 sample casts it at least once — the second most consistent gap this raid. Healing Stream Totem was never cast on any of the 10 kills, despite a real, if modest, Top 100 share on every boss (1.4%-9.3%, highest on Void Reaver) — a complete absence across the entire raid night, and an even more total version of the same gap already flagged on the 07-10 raid night (where it appeared on 1 of 9 kills). Vajomee died in 3 of the 10 kills (Lady Vashj, High Astromancer Solarian, Kael'thas Sunstrider) — a real, checkable fact that plausibly explains part of the below-average final output on at least two of those (Vashj and Solarian, where the death lands late/mid-cascade); see each page's own scorecard for the exact timing.
