Per-boss summary

HPS and overheal are real, computed from Danceswtrees's own fight logs. Percentile is pulled from the real WCL ranking for each exact report/fight ID — click a boss name for the full audit.

!
Overheal ran above the Top 10 median on 8 of the 10 kills tonight — often by 15 to 23 points — and tracks closely with a Lifebloom-heavy cast mix. On 8 of 10 bosses Lifebloom carried more of total healing than the Top 10 average, in several cases (Morogrim +30.5pts, Void Reaver +37.9pts, Al'ar +20.7pts) by a wide margin, with Regrowth and Rejuvenation correspondingly under-cast. The two kills that inverted this — Void Reaver and High Astromancer Solarian, both Regrowth-heavy relative to benchmark — produced two of the three best percentiles of the raid (39th and 83.8th) and two of the three lowest overheal numbers. That correlation shows up consistently enough to be worth a real look at Lifebloom refresh/uptime habits rather than treating each fight as independent variance — see each boss page's spell composition section for the exact numbers.
